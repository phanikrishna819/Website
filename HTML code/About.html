About us

Sarada Sarees is the most humble product we meet and live with every day. We established this in the year 2019, we manage almost 250+ members in business successfully. We sell a wide variety of sarees, such as kanchipattu, dharmavaram, benares, and more. Satisfying our customers by offering them the best quality sarees is our main aim. Our relationship with customers is improved because we directly source sarees from weavers.
